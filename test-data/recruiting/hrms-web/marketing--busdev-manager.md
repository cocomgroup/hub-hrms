# Marketing & Business Development Manager - Hospice Services

## About Us

We are a compassionate hospice care provider dedicated to delivering exceptional end-of-life care with dignity, comfort, and respect. Our mission is to support patients and their families during life's most challenging moments through comprehensive, person-centered hospice services. We are seeking a talented Marketing & Business Development Manager to help us expand our reach and connect more families with the care they need.

## Position Overview

**Title:** Marketing & Business Development Manager  
**Department:** Marketing & Business Development  
**Reports To:** Director of Operations / Regional Director  
**Employment Type:** Full-Time  
**Location:** [City, State] with travel to facilities and healthcare partners (up to 40% travel)  
**Salary Range:** $65,000 - $85,000 annually (commensurate with experience)

## Position Summary

The Marketing & Business Development Manager will be responsible for developing and maintaining strategic relationships with healthcare providers, facilities, and community organizations to drive hospice referrals and program growth. This role combines relationship-building, community education, and strategic marketing to increase awareness of our hospice services while ensuring compliance with healthcare marketing regulations.

## Key Responsibilities

### Business Development & Relationship Management (60%)
- Build and maintain relationships with physicians, hospitals, skilled nursing facilities, assisted living communities, and discharge planners
- Conduct regular face-to-face visits with referral sources to educate about hospice services and eligibility criteria
- Develop and implement territory management strategies to maximize referral opportunities
- Respond to inquiries from potential referral sources and facilitate smooth patient admissions
- Track referral patterns and identify opportunities for growth
- Attend hospital rounds, case conferences, and care coordination meetings as appropriate
- Serve as primary liaison between referral sources and clinical team

### Marketing & Community Outreach (30%)
- Plan and execute community education events, health fairs, and professional seminars
- Develop marketing materials including brochures, presentations, and educational content
- Coordinate with corporate marketing team on local campaigns and initiatives
- Maintain active presence in professional healthcare networks and associations
- Represent the organization at community events and speaking engagements
- Manage local social media presence and digital marketing efforts
- Create monthly reports on marketing activities, referral trends, and market intelligence

### Compliance & Documentation (10%)
- Ensure all marketing activities comply with Medicare regulations, HIPAA, and organizational policies
- Maintain accurate records of all business development activities in CRM system
- Document referral source interactions and follow-up activities
- Participate in quality assurance and compliance training
- Support regulatory audits and reviews as needed
